About Us

Welcome to the Cedula e-Service Portal of the Municipality of Leon! This online platform is designed to simplify the process of reserving and processing cedulas (residence certificates) for all residents.

With the Cedula e-Service Portal, residents can easily request and schedule appointments for their cedula issuance at their convenience. This eliminates long waiting times and makes the process more efficient. The system allows for seamless communication with the Municipal Office, where the requests will be reviewed and approved or rejected by authorized personnel.

Our goal is to enhance accessibility, speed, and convenience for residents, providing an easy way to handle this essential document while reducing manual processes for workers in the municipality.

Thank you for choosing the Cedula e-Service Portal — a faster, easier way to get your cedula in the Municipality of Leon!
